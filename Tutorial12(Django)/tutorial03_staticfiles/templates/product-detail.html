Product Description

Our Bamboo Toothbrush Set is the perfect sustainable alternative to plastic toothbrushes. Each set includes four toothbrushes with soft bristles that provide a gentle yet effective clean while being kind to your gums.

The handles are made from 100% organic bamboo, a fast-growing, sustainable resource that's naturally antibacterial. Unlike plastic toothbrushes that sit in landfills for centuries, our bamboo handles will biodegrade when properly disposed of in a composting environment.

We offer two bristle options: standard nylon bristles (which should be removed before composting the handle) or our premium plant-based bristles made from castor bean oil (fully biodegradable).
